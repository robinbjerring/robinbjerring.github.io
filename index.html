About me

A master of IT looking for work as a game designer, project manager or user researcher.

Design in IT is my favorite thing. I recently completed the DADIU course as a lead game designer (The National Academy of Digital, Interactive Entertainment). During my master of IT I wrote projects on esports economy, AI in games and lootboxes. My master thesis is about the percieved difficulty of video games. While games is of course a big part of my life, I also enjoyed doing lectures on cryptocurrency and blockchain for fellow students as well as through a blockchain network, I started. In 2018 I lead game design on the iOS and Android DADIU game Night Painter and at Nordic Game Jam 2016 my co-designers and me won an award for best board game. When I am not making games or talking about cryptocurrency, I write music and edit music. And then I read Batman comics.
